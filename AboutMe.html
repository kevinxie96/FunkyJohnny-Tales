Story Time
This is your author Kevin Xie. I am a second year Mathematics of Computation major attending the University of California, Los Angeles. This is a little web project I did combining my love for writing and my love for learning code. This website was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery. I learned some Front-End developing because I could not register for any C++ courses. All in all a good experience.

I am an avid Chess player and gamer. I used to play a lot of Dota2 but if any of you have ever played a MOBA you will know that it is one of the most time consuming hobbies to have. This slide show depicts some of my moments competing in the chess scholastic nationals and some miscellaneous photos of me as a kid. Also, feel free to turn on some John Williams. The Force Awakens just came out as I am writing this so get pumped and go see Star Wars.

Since this is my first About Me as an author, I will be succinct (the ego will come later, rest assured). Hope you enjoy this website. I hope to create some more in the future.
